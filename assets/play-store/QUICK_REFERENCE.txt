║          GOOGLE PLAY FEATURE GRAPHIC REQUIREMENTS            ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

📐 DIMENSIONS
   1024 x 500 pixels (exactly)

📄 FORMAT
   JPG or PNG

📊 ASPECT RATIO
   2:1

🎨 TRANSPARENCY
   NOT ALLOWED (must have solid background)

💾 FILE SIZE
   Maximum 15MB

🎯 PURPOSE
   • Featured by Google at top of Play Store listing
   • Promotional sections
   • Curated collections

✅ QUICK CHECKLIST
   □ Exactly 1024 x 500 pixels
   □ PNG or JPG format
   □ No transparency/alpha channel
   □ Under 15MB
   □ High quality, clear visuals
   □ Text readable at small sizes
   □ Brand consistent

🎨 DESIGN TIPS
   • Keep important content 50px from edges
   • Text minimum 80px from edges
   • Font size minimum 40px
   • Use high contrast
   • Include app name/logo
   • Keep text minimal

🚫 AVOID
   ✗ Transparency
   ✗ Device frames
   ✗ Excessive text
   ✗ Low resolution
   ✗ Misleading imagery
   ✗ Pricing info

📁 SAVE TO
   assets/play-store/feature-graphic.png

🔗 UPLOAD AT
   Google Play Console > Store presence > Main store listing > Graphics
